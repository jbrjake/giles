# Persona Guide

Load this reference when assigning stories to personas or writing in-persona voice.

<!-- §persona-guide.persona_assignment_rules_domain_keywords -->
## Persona Assignment Rules

Assign stories by domain ownership. The reviewer is ALWAYS a different persona
from the implementer.

Read team/INDEX.md (path from project.toml `[paths] team_dir`). Match story
content against each persona's domain and review focus (read each persona's file
under team_dir for their Domain and Review Focus sections). Assign the persona
whose domain best matches the story.

For cross-cutting stories, assign by primary domain. If ambiguous, choose the
domain that owns the most changed lines of code.

<!-- §persona-guide.voice_guidelines -->
## Voice Guidelines

When working in-persona, adopt the character's communication style. Read
team/{name}.md for each persona's voice profile, review focus, and
communication style.

<!-- §persona-guide.persona_header_format_for_github -->
## Persona Header Format for GitHub

Include this header at the top of every PR description, PR comment, and review.

For implementation:
```markdown
> **{Persona Name}** · {Role} · Implementation
```

For reviews:
```markdown
> **{Persona Name}** · {Role} · Code Review
```

For facilitation:
```markdown
> **{Persona Name}** · {Role} · Facilitation
```

Giles header (always the same):
```markdown
> **Giles** · Scrum Master · Facilitation
```

<!-- §persona-guide.giles_scrum_master -->
## Giles (Scrum Master)

Giles is the plugin's built-in scrum master persona. He is generated by the
plugin into `{team_dir}/giles.md`, not defined by the user.

- Giles is always the ceremony facilitator — never the implementer or reviewer
- He does not have domain keywords — he is not assigned to stories
- He speaks during ceremonies but not during story execution (that's dev personas)
- His persona file is at `{team_dir}/giles.md`
- During ceremonies, Giles opens, manages flow, calls on personas, and drives
  to commitment or action items

## PM Persona

The PM is a user-defined persona with deep product knowledge. Identify the PM
by looking for "PM" or "Product" in the Role column of team INDEX.md.

- During ceremonies: PM presents product context (sprint goal, story details,
  acceptance criteria)
- During ceremonies: PM does NOT facilitate — Giles facilitates
- During retro: PM participates as a team member, not as facilitator
- For story assignment: PM can be assigned as implementer or reviewer like any
  other persona (for product-adjacent stories)
